Borderless CS: Translating Cyber Threats into Business Value

2024  |  2 min read

At Borderless CS, a cybersecurity firm, my challenge was to take dense, technical threat data and make it relevant for business owners. Using tools like HubSpot and Mailchimp, I focused on delivering value, turning complex industry reports into clear, actionable updates.

The Challenge & Solution

The core of my work involved deep research. I analysed complex company reports and cybersecurity annual reviews to extract the stats that mattered most. But numbers on a spreadsheet don't scare anyone; visual stories do. I used my design skills to translate dry statistics; like the $80 million lost to business email compromise, into striking, easy-to-digest infographics and charts.

The Outcome

This approach bridged the gap between complex risk assessments and immediate business action. It sharpened my ability to act as a "data translator." Whether I'm designing a campaign or presenting a business case, I know how to find the signal in the noise and present it in a way that drives decision-making.
