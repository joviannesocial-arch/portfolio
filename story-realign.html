Realign Your Life: Full-Stack Digital Transformation

2024  |  2 min read

Working with a team of five, I helped transform the digital presence of 'Realign Your Life,' an Australian-based professional coaching business. This wasn't just a design refresh; it was a full-scale build including a client-facing portal and a robust admin backend. My role went beyond just "making it look good". I was the bridge between design and development, translating abstract ideas into concrete, testable functionality.

The Challenge & Solution

Clarity and simplicity is my definition of a successful platform. I connected the client's vision with the code, using tools like Jira, Miro, and LucidChart to turn abstract ideas into clear tasks for the developers. I mapped out the System Architecture and ERDs to ensure our database structure actually made sense, and I managed our Agile sprints to keep the team moving forward. I also took personal responsibility for Quality Assurance by writing rigorous Test Cases and conducting the UAT myself to catch bugs before we went live.

The Outcome

But the job didn't end at launch. I wanted the client to feel confident running her own business, so I wrote a comprehensive user manual guiding her through every feature of the website. This project proved that I can balance front-end creativity with the heavy lifting of technical documentation. It showed that I don't just understand the big picture, but I also have the discipline to handle the details that make it a reality.
